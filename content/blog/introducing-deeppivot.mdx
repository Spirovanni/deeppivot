# Introducing DeepPivot

Career pivots are hard. Whether you're transitioning industries, returning to the workforce, or leveling up after a bootcamp — the job search process can feel overwhelming. That's why we built **DeepPivot**: a platform that combines AI-powered interview coaching, career self-discovery, and hands-on job market tools in one place.

## Practice Interviews with a Real Voice Coach

At the heart of DeepPivot is **Sarah** — our ElevenLabs Conversational AI voice coach. Sarah conducts live voice interviews with you in real time, asking behavioral, technical, and situational questions tailored to your target role. After each session, you get:

- An **emotional intelligence report** measuring confidence, clarity, and adaptability
- A **performance score** tracking your improvement over time
- **Actionable feedback** on communication patterns, specific answers, and areas to practice

No more rehearsing answers alone in a mirror. Practice with an AI that responds naturally, follows up on your answers, and gives you honest feedback.

## Discover Your Career Archetype

Not sure where your skills best fit? DeepPivot's AI analyzes patterns in your interview responses and behavioral traits to assign you a **Career Archetype** — a profile of your working style, natural strengths, and growth areas.

Your archetype isn't a box — it's a starting point. Use it to explore career directions you may not have considered, or to identify what to emphasize in your next interview.

## Build a Career Plan You Can Actually Follow

The **Career Plan Builder** lets you create a draggable, milestone-based roadmap. Attach resources (articles, courses, certifications) to each milestone, track completion, and share your plan with a mentor or career coach.

For Workforce Development Board (WDB) clients, your IEP goals can sync directly into your career plan — giving you a clear path from training to employment.

## Track Every Job Application in One Place

The **Job Tracker** is a Kanban board for every application you're managing. Add job cards, move them through stages (Applied → Phone Screen → Interview → Offer), and never lose track of where you are in the process.

When you apply through the DeepPivot **Job Marketplace**, your tracker is updated automatically.

## A Marketplace That Works for Both Sides

Employers can post jobs and connect directly with learners who are actively building skills on the platform. Learners see a "✦ Via DeepPivot" badge on cards sourced from the marketplace — a signal that these employers value growth-mindset candidates.

## What's Next

We're just getting started. On the roadmap:

- **Advanced analytics** — deeper session-over-session insights
- **Mentor matching** — connect with industry mentors who've been where you want to go
- **Group interview simulation** — practice with peers or an employer-facilitated panel
- **International support** — i18n for Spanish, Portuguese, and French
